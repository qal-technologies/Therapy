Payment Instructions

To approve or decline a payment, swipe left on the payment message or click the reply button, then type one of the following keywords followed by your message:


  • approved - Marks the payment as successful.
  • declined - Marks the payment as failed.

Always add a message when you mark payment as declined or failed. Keywords like Incomplete, Incorrect, and Used are necessary for getting and updating the right payment response to the user.

Example: approved Payment confirmed. The book is now available.
