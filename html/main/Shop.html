THE BOOK

In the half-light of a forgotten coastal town, where the tide seems to swallow secrets and time has a strange way of folding in on itself, a reclusive watchmaker named Elias Varn begins to question his own reality. When an old journal washes ashore—its pages filled with impossible entries written in his own handwriting—Elias is drawn into a decades-old mystery involving a missing child, a love that defied death, and a town that remembers what people forget.

As the fog thickens and past and present begin to blur, Elias must confront the darkest corners of his mind and the one question he has long feared: what if the version of his life he remembers isn’t the truth?

A haunting, lyrical exploration of memory, guilt, and the strange ways grief can time-travel, The Hour Between Shadows will stay with you long after the final page is turned.
