WHY HEALING WITH CHARLOTTE CASIRAGHI

Join thousands of mental health professionals, relationship experts, and curious minds on April 25–26, 2025 , for Charlotte Casiraghi’s eighth-annual training conference, Sessions Live 2025: Mating in the Metacrisis: Connection, Polarization, and Eroticism in a World on Edge.

Together with Charlotte and her most trusted colleagues, you’ll discover new ways to connect in a rapidly shifting world, plus:
• Invaluable insights into the impacts of technology on relationships and social behavior
• Practical strategies for combating modern challenges and fostering in-person connection

• A special supervision segment, where you’ll get to see Esther and her esteemed colleagues discuss, analyze, and critique moments from real case materials

Whether you’re a practicing clinician, a personal growth enthusiast, or just interested in human relationships, you’ll find new perspectives and actionable insights to start improving your professional and personal relationships at Sessions Live 2025.
