We are deeply committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our website and services.
1. Information We Collect
We collect information you provide directly when you:
Register for a session, course, or product Make a donation or purchase Send a message, email, or support request Subscribe to our newsletter This may include your name, email address, phone number, billing information, and personal notes or reflections shared during your journey.
2. How We Use Your Information We use your information to: Process bookings and purchases Send confirmations and personalized updates Deliver voice messages or session details Offer emotional support and follow-ups Improve our services and offerings

We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.
3. Payment Information
All payments are securely processed using encrypted third-party systems (such as PayPal, Stripe, or verified bank transfer platforms). We do not store your full payment details.
4. Data Security
We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your information. Access to any sensitive data is restricted and monitored.
5. Confidentiality of Sessions
All one-on-one and group sessions are confidential. Anything you share with Charlotte remains private and protected. Only you and Charlotte have access to your session content.
6. Email & Messaging
By subscribing or registering, you agree to receive occasional updates from Charlotte, which may include audio messages, reflections, or upcoming offers. You can opt out at any time.
7. Cookies
Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze traffic. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.
8. Your Rights
You may request access to your data, correction of inaccuracies, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.
9. Contact
For privacy-related questions, email us at: healingwithcharlottecasiraghi@gmail.com
We honor your trust and treat your presence here with care. This space is sacred, and your story is safe with us.
